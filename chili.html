Fila's Cookbook

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Sirloin Steak - or Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork Sausage
  • Masa
  • Ancho Chile Powder
  • Cayenne
  • Cumin
  • Dried Oregano
  • Onions & Garlic
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Brown Sugar
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Pinto or Kidney Beans
  • Mild Flavor Beer

Directions

  1. Saute Veggies - heat oil and add onions, peppers, carrots, and garlic. Saute for 5 minutes.
  2. brown the meat - Add steak and sausage and cook until brown.
  3. Add spices & liquids - Add masa, Ancho chili powder, Cumin, Oregano, Salt, Sugar, Tomatoes, Beans (drained), and beer. Scrape to deglaze.
  4. Simmer - reduce heat and cook for 30 minutes.
