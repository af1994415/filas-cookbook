Fila's Cookbook

Ingredients

  • At least 3 types of cheese - 1 soft (Montery Jack), 1 flavorful (Chedder), 1 Funky (Parmesian), add more to taste
  • Fat - butter, olive oil, ...
  • Whole Milk
  • AP Flour
  • Pasta - Macaroni, Penne, ...
  • Salt & Pepper - to tase

Directions

  1. Combine equal parts flour and fat to create a blond roux
  2. Once roux is created add in milk to create a beshemel
  3. Add grated cheese until melted
  4. At the same time cook pasta to al dente
  5. Combine cheese sauce and pasta and cook to finish
  6. Optionaly, cover Mac & Cheese in panko bread crumbs and additional cheese and broil until golden brown
