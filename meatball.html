Fila's Cookbook

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef - 1lb
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Egg - 1
  • White Sandwich Bread - 2 slices
  • Garlic - 1.5 cloves
  • Parsley - 1/8 cup chopped
  • Pecorino - 1/8-1/4 cup
  • Ricotta - 1 heaped tbsp
  • Zuchinni - 1 heaped tbsp

Directions

  1. Let bread soak in milk for a couple of minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 425F
  3. Combine all ingredients
  4. Cook for 16-18 minutes
