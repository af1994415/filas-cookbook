Fila's Cookbook

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef - 1 lb
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Publano pepper
  • Smoked Paprika - 1 tbsp
  • Ancho Chili Powder - 1 tbsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tbsp
  • Cumin - 1 tbsp
  • Corriander - 1 tsp
  • Dried Thime - 1 tsp
  • Cayenne Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Beef stock - 1/2 cup

Directions

  1. heat cast iron pan over medium heat
  2. Add olive oil, onion, garlic, and black pepper and let cook for a few minutes
  3. Char and breakdown publano peper, add it to the pan
  4. In a seperate bowl combine spices
  5. Add beef to the pan one onions are translucent, salt
  6. Once beef is cooked half way add in a couple tablespoons of spice mix
  7. When beef is cooked add in beef stock
  8. Serve
